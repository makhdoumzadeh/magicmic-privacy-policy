# Privacy Policy for MagicMic

Last updated: December 2025

MagicMic respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
- Audio recordings are temporarily processed to perform speech-to-text and translation.
- No audio recordings are stored permanently.
- Anonymous device identifiers may be used to manage usage credits.

## How We Use Information
- To provide voice transcription and translation services.
- To manage purchased translation credits.

## Data Sharing
- Audio data is sent securely to third-party AI services for processing.
- We do not sell or share personal data for advertising purposes.

## Data Retention
- Audio data is deleted immediately after processing.
- No personal conversations are stored.

## User Rights
You may stop using the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.
