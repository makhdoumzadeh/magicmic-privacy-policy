Delete Data Request – MagicMic

MagicMic does not require users to create an account.

How to request deletion

Email us at: makhdoumzadeh@gmail.com

Subject line: Delete my MagicMic data

Please include:

  • Your approximate request date/time
  • Your device ID (if available)
  • Your country (optional)

If you do not have this information, you can still request deletion. We will process your request using the information available to us.

What data can be deleted

  • Server-side credits associated with your device
  • Any server logs that can reasonably be linked to your request

Retention

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Some minimal records may be retained if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.
